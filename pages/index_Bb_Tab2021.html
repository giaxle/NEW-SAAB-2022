ABOUT THE STUDENT ADVISORY BOARD


The Student & Alumni Advisory Board (SAAB) is a diverse and accomplished collective of current students appointed to represent their community within the Jack Welch Management Institute. Its mission is to provide student and alumni advocacy, foster community and support student success. Board members will serve as the voice of their peers, helping to improve the experience for all students and alumni. Serving on the Board also provides members with the opportunity for increased engagement with their cohort, students and alumni in different phases of their academic career, and with JWMI leadership and staff.
