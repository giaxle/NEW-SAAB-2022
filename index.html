The Client Student and Alumni Advisory Board’s mission is to provide student and alumni advocacy, foster community, support student success, and promote the Client brand. We are looking for new Board Members who will drive our mission forward and apply the client's 4Es and a P during every interaction.


2022 APPLICATION PROCESS

We are currently accepting applications for open Board Member positions! To qualify, applicants must meet the following requirements:

  • Currently enrolled students who have completed one course as of the Winter term and have a current GPA of 3.26 or higher, or Alumni with a with a cumulative GPA of 3.26 or higher.
  • Be in good standing with Client
  • Include a referral from a faculty member or a current or former SAAB member
  • Submit a complete application
  • Commit to a one-year term (March – March)
  • Actively participate in monthly Board meetings and ongoing initiatives

  • Voting Period: February 14th - February 18th
